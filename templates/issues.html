Issues

Sridhar will continue to work on various people friendly and anti-corruption initiatives as he has been doing successfully in the last 5 years.

Electing Sridhar Pabbisetty as your MLA will help him better implement the following fundamental but crisp agenda:

Governance

  • All Government of Karnataka Services will come under Sakala with a specific time limit and transparent process
  • Modernization and Simplification of steps undertaken to ensure easier and faster delivery of services by government

Impetus to Local governance

  • Greater power to local corporations, municipalities, panchayats, and citizens.
  • Representative Ward Committees that can oversee local developmental plans.
  • Assembly Constituency oversight committee that will develop and coordinate plans at the Assembly level.

Employment and Small Businesses

  • Job oriented courses for the unemployed and underemployed youths.
  • Making it easier for Small businesses to operate

Planned Growth

  • Integrated planning and zoning for developing commercial, industrial and residential clusters
  • Planning Schools, Hospitals and other necessary infrastructure at a reasonable distance
  • Emphasis on developing tier-2 and tier-3 cities and developing entreprenuerial ecosystems

Address Corruption and Bad Governance

  • Enforcing compliance of Section 4.1.a and 4.1.b among all public institutions
  • Greater Transparency and Accountability and reduced discretion in decision making

Well Being

  • Reliable and safe public transport
  • Safety and security of women and senior citizens
  • Decentralized and scientific waste management
  • Enforcing the Rule of Law
